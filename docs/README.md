# Crown Valet Documentation

Crown Valet is a connected valet parking and vehicle services platform. The product combines a customer mobile experience, valet team workflow tools, venue dashboards, partner service fulfillment, payments, notifications, and analytics.

This documentation package is designed for business stakeholders, venue partners, and the development team. Start with the product documents to understand the vision, then move into operations and technical planning.

## Product Modules

- Customer mobile app for valet ticketing, car tracking, service requests, payment, and pickup.
- Valet operations app for check-in, vehicle status, parking location, pickup queue, and runner coordination.
- Venue dashboard for operational visibility, revenue, performance, and issue tracking.
- Admin platform for configuration, roles, pricing, reporting, support, and audit logs.
- Service marketplace for cleaning, detailing, gas fill-up, EV charging, and partner fulfillment.
- Notification system for SMS, push, and email updates.
- Payment system for valet fees, tips, add-ons, refunds, subscriptions, and partner revenue share.
