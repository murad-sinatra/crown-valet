# Feature Catalog

## Customer Mobile App

### Account and Profile

- Sign up with phone, email, or social login.
- Store contact preferences and notification consent.
- Manage saved vehicles with make, model, color, plate, photos, and notes.
- Save payment methods for valet fees, tips, and add-ons.
- View past visits, receipts, and service history.

### Digital Valet Ticket

- Receive a digital ticket by QR code, SMS link, app notification, or email.
- View ticket number, venue, check-in time, vehicle details, and status.
- Share ticket access with another trusted person when pickup authorization is needed.
- Recover ticket access through verified phone or email.

### Car Status and Tracking

- Show status such as checked in, being parked, parked, service in progress, pickup requested, retrieving, ready, and completed.
- Display approximate parking zone or location when allowed by venue policy.
- Show timestamps for major events.
- Display pickup ETA when available.
- Notify customer if the vehicle is delayed, blocked, or requires support.

### Pickup Request

- Request vehicle pickup from the app.
- Choose immediate pickup or scheduled pickup when supported by the venue.
- Show queue position or estimated wait time.
- Allow cancellation or modification until retrieval begins.
- Notify the customer when the car is on the way or ready.

### Add-On Services

- Browse available services by venue and vehicle eligibility.
- Request car wash, interior cleaning, detailing, gas fill-up, EV charging, tire pressure check, or other partner services.
- See price, estimated completion time, and service requirements.
- Receive service status updates and proof of completion.
- Add multiple services to the same valet session when operationally feasible.

### Payments and Receipts

- Pay valet fees, add-on services, and tips digitally.
- Support venue-paid, customer-paid, subscription, voucher, and corporate account models.
- Show itemized receipts.
- Handle refunds, partial refunds, and failed payments.
- Support promotional codes and partner credits.

### Support and Incidents

- Contact support from an active or past valet session.
- Report missing item, damage concern, service issue, or billing issue.
- Attach photos and notes.
- View support status and resolution updates.

## Valet Operations App

### Vehicle Check-In

- Create valet session from QR scan, license plate, manual entry, or pre-booking.
- Capture customer contact details and consent.
- Capture vehicle details, mileage when needed, fuel level when needed, and special handling notes.
- Take exterior and interior condition photos.
- Assign key tag and ticket number.

### Parking Workflow

- Assign runner or parking team.
- Record parking zone, floor, row, stall, or GPS-assisted location.
- Update status when vehicle is parked.
- Add internal notes for blocked-in cars, oversized vehicles, restricted access, or VIP handling.

### Pickup Workflow

- View pickup queue sorted by request time, priority, venue rules, or VIP status.
- Assign runner to retrieve the vehicle.
- Track retrieval progress.
- Mark vehicle ready for customer handoff.
- Confirm final handoff and close the session.

### Staff Coordination

- Role-specific views for greeter, runner, manager, service partner, and admin.
- Shift assignment and staff activity tracking.
- Internal notifications for delays, incidents, or priority pickups.
- Escalation workflow for lost keys, blocked cars, customer complaints, or service failures.

## Venue Dashboard

### Live Operations

- View active vehicles by status.
- Monitor average check-in and pickup wait time.
- See current pickup queue and delayed vehicles.
- View add-on service progress.
- Filter by shift, staff member, zone, event, or venue area.

### Revenue and Performance

- Track valet fees, tips, add-on services, partner revenue share, refunds, and net revenue.
- Compare volume and revenue by day, week, month, event, and location.
- Track service conversion rate and customer satisfaction.
- Identify peak demand windows and staffing needs.

### Customer Experience

- Monitor complaints, incidents, support requests, and service ratings.
- Review customer communication history.
- Configure branded messages and pickup instructions.
- Manage VIP, resident, employee, or corporate account rules.

## Admin Platform

### Venue Configuration

- Create and manage venues, zones, parking areas, teams, services, and operating hours.
- Configure pricing, taxes, service fees, tip options, and refund rules.
- Enable or disable features per venue.
- Manage branding, logos, colors, and customer-facing copy.

### Roles and Permissions

- Manage users and staff roles.
- Restrict access by venue, function, and permission level.
- Audit sensitive actions such as refunds, status overrides, incident edits, and payment changes.

### Service Catalog

- Define service types, prices, service windows, partner assignment rules, and availability.
- Configure vehicle eligibility and customer instructions.
- Manage proof requirements such as photos, mileage, fuel receipt, or charging session confirmation.

### Reporting and Support

- Search across customers, vehicles, tickets, payments, and incidents.
- Export reports for venues, partners, finance, and operations.
- Manage disputes, refunds, and account support.
- Review audit logs.

## Service Partner Tools

- Receive service jobs with vehicle details, parking location, timing window, and instructions.
- Accept, start, pause, complete, or reject service jobs.
- Upload proof photos and notes.
- Coordinate with valet team when a vehicle must be moved.
- View completed jobs and payout reconciliation.

## Notifications

- Send SMS, push, and email updates based on customer preference and venue policy.
- Trigger notifications for check-in, parked, service started, service completed, pickup requested, retrieval started, ready, receipt, delay, and support updates.
- Provide staff notifications for new pickup requests, service issues, delayed vehicles, and escalation events.

## Analytics

- Track operational, financial, and customer experience metrics.
- Support venue-level, partner-level, and platform-level reporting.
- Identify bottlenecks such as slow check-in, high pickup wait times, service delays, or frequent disputes.
- Provide launch dashboards for new venue onboarding.
