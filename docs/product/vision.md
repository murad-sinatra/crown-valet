# Product Vision

## Summary

Crown Valet is a premium valet parking platform that gives customers visibility and control over their vehicle while helping venues run faster, cleaner, and more profitable valet operations.

The product begins with a simple promise: when someone hands over their keys, they should know where their car is, what is happening to it, when it will be ready, and what extra services they can request before pickup.

## Problem

Traditional valet operations are often manual, opaque, and reactive. Customers receive a paper ticket, wait without visibility, and only interact again when they want the car returned. Venues and valet operators rely on radios, handwritten notes, and staff memory to manage vehicle flow.

This creates several problems:

- Customers do not know whether their car has been parked, serviced, or queued for pickup.
- Pickup wait times can feel unpredictable and frustrating.
- Venues miss revenue from high-intent add-on services such as cleaning, detailing, fuel, and EV charging.
- Managers lack real-time visibility into staff performance, delays, incidents, and customer satisfaction.
- Damage claims and disputes are difficult to resolve without timestamped photos and audit history.
- Partner services are hard to coordinate without a shared fulfillment workflow.

## Product Mission

Crown Valet turns valet parking into a connected service experience. The platform should make every vehicle handoff traceable, every customer interaction convenient, and every venue operation measurable.

## Target Customers

Crown Valet is designed for venues and operators where valet parking is part of the guest experience:

- Hotels and resorts
- Restaurants and nightlife venues
- Event spaces and stadiums
- Luxury residential buildings
- Hospitals and medical centers
- Airports and private terminals
- Corporate campuses
- Shopping centers and mixed-use developments

## Value Proposition

### For Customers

- Know when the car is checked in, parked, being serviced, and ready.
- Request pickup from the phone before arriving at the valet stand.
- Add services such as cleaning, gas fill-up, or EV charging while the car is already parked.
- Pay, tip, and receive receipts digitally.
- Access support and proof of vehicle condition if an issue occurs.

### For Venues

- Improve the guest experience with a modern, branded valet flow.
- Reduce confusion at peak pickup times.
- Generate incremental revenue from vehicle services and tips.
- Monitor operational performance in real time.
- Use reporting to optimize staffing, pricing, and service offerings.

### For Valet Operators

- Replace paper tickets and manual status tracking with a clear workflow.
- Improve coordination between greeters, parkers, runners, managers, and service partners.
- Reduce disputes with photo capture, timestamps, and audit logs.
- Measure team performance and identify bottlenecks.

### For Service Partners

- Receive structured service requests with vehicle details, location, timing, and proof requirements.
- Update fulfillment status inside the same platform.
- Build repeat business through venue partnerships.

## Product Principles

- Trust first: customers and venues need clear evidence for status, location, service completion, and incidents.
- Fast handoffs: the product should reduce friction at check-in and pickup, not add steps.
- Operational clarity: staff should always know which vehicle needs attention next.
- Premium by default: tone, flows, and visual presentation should feel appropriate for luxury and hospitality environments.
- Configurable for venues: pricing, services, branding, pickup rules, and staff roles should adapt by location.
- Practical tracking: location should be useful for operations and customer confidence while respecting privacy and feasibility.

## Success Metrics

- Average check-in time
- Average pickup wait time
- Percentage of cars with complete status history
- Percentage of customers using digital pickup
- Add-on service conversion rate
- Average revenue per valet session
- Tip conversion and average tip
- Service completion on-time rate
- Incident rate and dispute resolution time
- Venue retention and expansion rate

## Product Positioning

Crown Valet is not only a valet ticketing tool. It is a hospitality operations platform that connects parking, vehicle services, payments, and real-time customer communication into one branded experience.
