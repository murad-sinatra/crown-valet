# Crown Valet Stakeholder Deck

## Slide 1: Title

### Crown Valet

Connected valet parking and vehicle services for modern venues.

Presented for venue partners, operators, and business stakeholders.

## Slide 2: The Problem

Valet parking is often the first and last impression of a venue, yet the experience is still mostly manual.

- Customers wait without knowing vehicle status.
- Paper tickets are easy to lose and hard to audit.
- Pickup queues become stressful during rush periods.
- Venues miss revenue from services customers would buy if offered at the right moment.
- Damage claims and disputes are hard to resolve without digital records.

## Slide 3: The Opportunity

When a customer hands over their keys, the venue temporarily owns a high-trust service moment.

Crown Valet turns that moment into:

- Better guest communication
- Faster operations
- Digital payment and tipping
- Add-on vehicle service revenue
- Operational data for managers
- A premium branded experience

## Slide 4: The Solution

Crown Valet is a digital valet platform that connects customers, valet teams, venues, service partners, payments, and notifications.

Customers can track their car, request pickup, pay, tip, and add services. Valet teams can manage check-in, parking, retrieval, and incidents. Venues can see performance, revenue, and customer experience in real time.

## Slide 5: Customer Experience

1. Arrive at venue and hand off keys.
2. Receive digital valet ticket.
3. See vehicle status and parking progress.
4. Request cleaning, detailing, gas, or EV charging.
5. Request pickup before reaching the valet stand.
6. Pay, tip, and receive receipt digitally.
7. Access support if an issue occurs.

## Slide 6: Valet Team Experience

Crown Valet gives staff a clear operational workflow:

- Fast vehicle check-in
- QR or digital ticket creation
- Condition photo capture
- Parking location assignment
- Pickup queue management
- Runner assignment
- Incident and delay notes
- Final handoff confirmation

## Slide 7: Venue Dashboard

Venue teams gain visibility into:

- Active vehicles by status
- Pickup wait times
- Queue length
- Staff activity
- Service revenue
- Tips
- Customer issues
- Incident history
- Daily and event-based reports

## Slide 8: Add-On Services

Crown Valet creates revenue from services that fit naturally while a vehicle is parked.

Initial service opportunities:

- Exterior wash
- Interior cleaning
- Premium detailing
- Gas fill-up
- EV charging
- Tire pressure check
- Light maintenance referrals

## Slide 9: Partner Service Flow

1. Customer selects a service from the active valet ticket.
2. Service job is created with vehicle, location, timing, and instructions.
3. Staff or partner accepts the job.
4. Service status updates are shared with customer and valet team.
5. Completion proof is uploaded.
6. Revenue is reconciled between customer, venue, partner, and platform.

## Slide 18: Risks and Mitigation

- Staff adoption: design fast role-specific workflows and provide training.
- Peak demand reliability: optimize pickup queue and status updates first.
- Service quality: require proof of completion and partner SLAs.
- Privacy: limit exact vehicle location by role and venue policy.
- Payments: use established providers and clear receipts.
